Pure Sourcing Process

1. Location Identification

We select remote, unpolluted locations like the Swiss Alps, known for their exceptional air quality and pristine natural environment.

2. Environmental Assessment

Conduct thorough evaluations to confirm the purity, sustainability, and quality of the air source before collection.

3. Air Collection

Utilize specialized equipment to capture air at optimal times without contamination, preserving its natural purity.

4. Initial Filtration

Remove any particulates and impurities immediately after collection to maintain the highest level of air purity.

Quality Testing Process

1. Purity Analysis

Test for contaminants and pollutants to ensure the air meets our strict purity standards and quality benchmarks.

2. Pressure Testing

Verify can integrity to withstand various conditions without leakage, ensuring product safety and longevity.

3. Seal Inspection

Ensure airtight seals are perfect to maintain freshness and prevent contamination throughout the product's lifecycle.

4. Batch Sampling

Randomly select cans from each batch for comprehensive quality assurance tests to maintain consistency across all products.

Premium Packaging Process

1. Design Planning

Crafting a concept that aligns with BreatheFree's premium brand identity and ensures optimal protection for our canned air products.

2. Material Selection

Choosing high-quality, sustainable materials that enhance product appeal and maintain air purity during storage and transit.

3. Secure Assembly

Precisely sealing each can in protective cases with reinforced corners and secure latches to ensure freshness and integrity.

4. Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality checks ensure every package meets our high standards before being prepared for delivery to customers.

Fresh Delivery Process

1. Order Processing
